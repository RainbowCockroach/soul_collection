Naame was born and raised in a vrev reserve on the planet Lunareth. From a young age, Naame and other vrev were taught to grow up in tourism, serving and entertaining bylunes. Naame hated this, feeling it demeaned him, as bylunes now viewed vrevs merely as beautiful dancing peacocks, almost no longer considered intelligent.

He befriended Iggy, a vrev of the same age, and they became very close, like brothers. Naame, yearning for freedom, persuaded Iggy to join him in his determination to go to school so they could find work later.  However, after experiencing many economic and emotional hardships, Iggy failed and gave up his dream of freedom, leaving Naame to fend for himself. Soon after, their friendship also broke down due to Naame's inability to control himself. During this time, he sought out and studied with his neighbor, Wsst, and discovered the old spaceship he had hidden.

After much effort, Naame passed the entrance exam to Kalcelin Medical School, the medical school with the lowest admission requirements in the city. He studied for several years while also working part-time at a nearby convenience store to pay for his tuition. However, one day, after a stressful day at work, coupled with frustration from a lack of affection and sex, Naame committed an unimaginably terrible act against Uaawa's younger brother. In a panic upon realizing what he had done, Naame stole Wsst's spaceship and fled to Earth.

Because the spaceship was still undergoing repairs, Naame lost control and was unable to steer it. Upon entering Earth's atmosphere, he made a sudden landing and fell to the South Pole. He used an emergency flare and was fortunately rescued.  However, these people were also there to experiment with illegal things. After learning about his background and why he was there, their superiors decided to sell him to Sa Pharma. Seana was the one who directly bought him, raising him as a laboratory pet, which angered Naame greatly. Along the way, recognizing her pet's talent, Seana used her connections to get him a job in the same laboratory as her. With no way out due to his criminal record and arrest warrant, Naame agreed.

Shortly after getting a job at Sa Pharma, Naame met Zorl and they became close friends. Zorl introduced Naame to his friend's house because apparently Naame didn't want to live with Seana. Seana, now trusting enough, let Naame go free.  Naame felt like his dream had finally come true, he had a job and a place of his own, no longer dependent on anyone.

Some time later, Naame met Non in the recovery room at the laboratory where he worked. They got to know each other and fell deeply in love. They dated for two years and proposed to each other. However, due to personal feelings and a jealous nature, Naame once attacked and harmed several of his colleagues, causing him to flee again, this time taking Non with him.
